Award Eligibility

To be eligible for our awards, nominees must meet the following criteria:

  • Nominees must be actively involved in one of our focus areas.
  • Nominees must demonstrate measurable impact within their community.
  • Nominees must be nominated by an eligible entity or individual.
  • Nominees must provide supporting documentation and references.
