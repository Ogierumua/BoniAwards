Best of Nigerian Industry Awards

Celebrating Excellence Across Nigerian Industries

About Us

The Best of Nigerian Industry Awards - BONI Awards have been created to recognise and celebrate excellence across various industries in Nigeria. It is an annual all-encompassing awards program which recognizes Industry Leaders and corporate organisations that demonstrate the core values of industry standards and innovation, employee engagement, customer satisfaction, experience management, business sustainability, product and technology innovation.

The awards serve as a platform to showcase innovation, leadership, and transformative industry practices that contribute to the economic development of Nigeria. The Best of Nigerian Industry Awards - BONI Awards, formerly known as the Nigerian National Legacy Awards, was established in 2009. It is driven by a full-fledged advisory board consisting of senior industry leaders, prominent bureaucrats, famous journalists, renowned corporate CEOs, industry experts, consultants, and subject matter specialists, all working to make Nigeria proud on national and international levels.

The awards celebrate the diligent work and persistence of booming industry leaders and honor the brands that have had a profound impact across various sectors of the Nigerian economy. We recognize the contribution of people, processes, and products in driving the industry forward to achieve success in a competitive market. By celebrating achievements and best industry practices, these awards inspire a culture of excellence and encourage continuous improvement in Nigeria's economic ecosystem.

The BONI Awards were instituted to identify and celebrate innovative individuals and enterprises across industry segments and sectors. The award gives corporate bodies the opportunity to shine and be recognized nationally and internationally for their innovative products and services, as well as their positive impact on society.
