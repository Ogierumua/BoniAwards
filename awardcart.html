BONI Awards Categories

Recognizing outstanding achievements in Nigeria

The Best of Nigeria's Industry Awards

The BONI Awards aims to recognize outstanding achievements by corporate bodies and individuals that directly or indirectly impact the socio-economic welfare of Nigeria. The BONI Awards is bestowed upon for outstanding achievements under the following Award Titles:

i. Business Transformation Award

Business must change. Our planet is on the brink of irreversible collapse and increasingly society expects companies to take the lead and address these social and environmental challenges. The Business Transformation Award will reward the individual/organization that is taking critical steps to transform their business model in line with a clean, resilient and just future.

ii. Product Innovation Award

The award will go to a company/Individual that has developed an innovative product offering that is helping address a critical social or environmental challenge. The winning entry will provide data, disclosures and investments that demonstrate real positive impact from this new product offering.

iii. Industry Sustainability Award

This award celebrates a person who is a global leader and pioneering new ideas and actions which challenge the status quo within their business and industry.

iv. Circular Transition Award

The award will go to a company/individual that has adopted a technology, initiative or project that is helping the business move from a linear to a circular model.

v. Business Transparency Award

This award will go out to a company that has demonstrated honest, transparent disclosure on both negative and positive impacts through their sustainability reporting process.

vi. Stakeholder Engagement Award

This category recognizes any communications initiative that has delivered measurable, positive and impactful social and environmental change among a key stakeholder group.

vii. Social Impact Award

This award will go to a company or individual that can demonstrate measurable social impact and positive change brought about by its ongoing corporate practice.

viii. Diversity, Equity & Inclusion Award

This award recognizes the hard work being undertaken to recruit and foster a diverse workforce.

ix. Driving Social Change Award

We will be recognizing those institutions which are demonstrating measurable social impact and positive change brought about by corporate practices.

x. CEO of the Year Award

This award honors an individual leading a company within Governance, risk and compliance to new innovative heights.

xi. Best Completion Technology Award

This award recognizes the leading solution that provided a substantial impact on well completion.

xii. Best Deepwater Technology Award

This award recognizes significant technology innovation in deepwater drilling, completions or production.

xiii. Lifetime Achievement Award

This award recognizes the accomplishments of outstanding individuals that have significantly advanced the energy industry.

xiv. Industry Person of the Year

This award recognizes an individual who has consistently exemplified the highest standards of professional excellence.

xv. Corporate Social Responsibility Award

This award celebrates excellence and innovation in corporate social responsibility, honoring outstanding achievements dedicated to making a positive impact on society.

xvi. Innovative Industry Strategy & Leadership Awards

This award recognizes and celebrates the achievements of leaders who are pushing boundaries and shaping the future of their industries.

xvii. Quality Excellence Awards

This award is given to an individual who fully recognizes the importance of quality and places great value on quality management and assurance.

xviii. Environmental Leadership Awards

This award recognizes individuals who consistently demonstrate commitment to progressive environmental practices.

xix. Health, Safety, Security, Environment and Social Responsibility Award

This award recognizes outstanding accomplishments in the field of environmental protection, health, or safety in oil and gas operations.

xx. Excellence In Ethical And Environmental Stewardship Award

This award recognizes individuals/organizations that have demonstrated stewardship by integrating environmental, economic, and social factors in their business strategies.

xxi. Environmental Stewardship, Preservation and Awareness

This award recognizes the establishment, improvement and/or maintenance of HSE practices by a team or focus group.

xxii. H.S.E. Evolution Award

This award recognizes changes that fundamentally enhance the Health, Safety, and Environmental system performance of the organization.

xxiii. Excellence in H.S.E Compliance Award

This award recognizes successful business models that uphold HSE as a key business value.

xxiv. Regulatory Excellence Award

This award recognizes an individual, team, program, or agency that contributes to enhancing occupational or professional regulation.

xxv. Consumer Protection Award

This award recognizes an individual who has innovatively contributed to enhancing public understanding of regulatory issues.
