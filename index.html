About Boni Awards

Objectives

  • To celebrate the best across the Nigerian Industrial Sector.
  • To recognize industry practices that catered to specific customer demands.
  • To recognize initiatives that have contributed to industry growth.
  • To reward innovations across the Nation's Industrial sector.
  • To celebrate change makers and innovators across segments.

Ideology

The liberalization of the Nigeria's Industries influenced by the remarkable performance of information technology, bio technology, agricultural advancement, communications and ground breaking innovations in the Oil and Gas reshaping the Nation's economic landscape with core purpose of reducing the role of the State , with industries emerging as a fuel to the nation's economy.

Our Approach

Gathering Nominees

Nominees for BONI Awards are gained via our online nomination form from our website and Social media platforms.

Evaluation & Screening

All those who formally accept their nomination will be asked if they would like to submit material for further consideration as supporting evidence. We consider materials supplied by nominees, information on those nominated available online and any information supplied with the original nomination.

Research & Judging

Our research team made up of an array of seasoned journalist and media experts will assess all information presented when it comes to determining winners. The team gather information independently from a number of publicly available sources, they will assess the material supplied by the nominees and by those who put them forward originally.

Contacting & Announcing the Winners

Once winners have been determined each winner will be contacted directly. Details of those winners will be embargoed until the BONI Awards formally announce the winners publicly via the website and our social media platforms.

Promote Your Win

We encourage all of our winners to publicly announce their win independently. Announcements via company websites, social media channels, via publicly released press releases, and through local and national press are agreat way of really maximising the exposure gained from such an amazing achievement.

Social Media Marketing

We use social media platforms to promote your content and spread your message in the most compelling way, engaging your target audience and building strong, long-term relationships

Why BONI Awards?

Being a recipient of the BONI Awards is a sure way to distinguish yourself from your competitors and achieve credibility and recognition from external influencers. From increasing brand exposure to strengthening business reputation, the awards bring several benefits:

Evaluation / Award Assessment Process

Assessors are provided with a summary of nominations and are required to declare any potential conflict of interest. If a conflict of interest is declared, the assessor will not be involved in any deliberations relating to that nomination.

Each nomination is reviewed by at least 5 assessors. If the assessors are unable to agree, an additional assessor reviews the nomination. If no nominations in a category meet the required standard, no Award will be given in that category.

Recommendations are submitted to the BONI Awards Board for approval, and the Board's decisions will be communicated to award recipients before formal announcements.

Award Eligibility / Criteria

  • Exhibit a history of service and leadership in their respective industry.
  • Must have a strong Corporate Social Responsibility record.
  • Must be innovative and willing to expand conventional industry practices.
  • Follow consistent ethical industry standards.
  • Must demonstrate a performance track-record over the past twelve months.

Frequently Asked Questions

i. How long has BONI Awards been in operation?

Since 2009, the BONI Awards have benchmarked Management Quality and industry standards in the Nigerian industrial and service sectors.

ii. Where do nominations come from?

Nominations are made by the Nigerian public. Your award will be received purely on merit, not based on votes.

iii. What happens if I agree to be shortlisted?

Nominees who accept their position will undergo internal vetting. They may provide their own data to be considered alongside the research team’s findings.

iv. Who makes the final decision?

A 15-man research panel conducts research and fact-checking to determine which individuals and companies are most deserving of the award.

v. Is there an Award Gala Night?

No, awards are presented individually at the recipients’ corporate headquarters to accommodate their busy schedules.

vi. Can I nominate myself?

Yes, self-nominations are welcome.
