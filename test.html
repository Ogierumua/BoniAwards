About Emerging Nigeria Consult Services

Who We Are

Emerging Nigeria Consult Services (ENCS) is the official organizer of the BONI Awards. We are your strategic communications and PR partner, helping you find synergy between your brand story and business objectives. Through our work, we manage, transform, and improve relations with the media, investors, clients, employees, and political stakeholders.

By leveraging media relations, research, public affairs, stakeholder engagement, digital content, and crisis management, we deliver influence with speed and intelligence. Our innovative strategies and ideas help build brands, increase awareness, and boost demand, providing real value to our clients. In times of adversity, we support clients to minimize damage and assist in recovery after crises.

Our Aim

At ENCS, our aim is to create, maintain, and protect our clients' reputations, enhance their prestige, and present a favorable image before the public.

How We Win

ENCS offers something most other firms cannot: an ability to influence online and offline conversations by rapidly producing content informed by data and intelligence. We achieve this in three key ways:

1. Content

We partner with you to develop a stream of original, paid, earned, and owned content that tells your story, controls the narrative, and strengthens your reputation.

2. Voices

We help you activate your own voice in public conversations and engage outside influencers to validate and amplify your message.

3. Distribution

We leverage paid, earned, and owned channels to deliver your content to the right audience at the right time on the right platforms.

Our Services

1. Campaigns

We create tailored campaigns with memorable storytelling that cut through media noise to engage your target audience.

2. Brand Communications & Activation

We drive brand growth through all consumer experiences, increasing awareness, visibility, and credibility among key stakeholders.

3. Brand Ranking & Evaluation

We assess and rank competitive brands, drawing focus to our clients through public perception assessments.

4. Digital Communication

We craft digital strategies that combine paid, earned, and owned channels to meet customers where and when they are open to your message.

5. Business Development

Our team creates memorable digital experiences and executes business development strategies, covering everything from influencer marketing to VIP events.

6. Social Media Marketing

We build strong social media presences to promote your content, build long-term relationships, and grow your brand.

7. Field Services & Stakeholder Engagement

We establish networks of external voices to lend credibility to your messaging and execute grassroots campaigns at the national level.

8. Media Relations

We leverage well-established media relationships to secure placements in top-tier outlets and generate diverse media coverage for your brand.

Our Leadership

Ovie Odubu, CEO

Ovie Odubu is a media insider with a talent for creating messages that differentiate and drive awareness. With years of experience directing media and publicity programs for global and national clients, Ovie brings innovative strategies to support client goals.

His approach is supported by a career in journalism, including time as a staff reporter for the Nigerian Television Authority. Over the past 13 years, Ovie has maintained connections across Nigeria and internationally, leading expansion, change processes, and corporate restructuring.
